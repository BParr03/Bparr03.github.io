About Me

General

I have worked with both front-end and back-end applications, which has greatly helped me improve my knowledge in how web applications work and how both ends work together to create a functioning and efficient website. I enjoy the creative aspect of front-end development, making designs as refined as possible and the problem-solving parts of working with the back-end.


Education
